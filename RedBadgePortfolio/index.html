A Bit About Me


I was born in Northeast Indiana and pride myself on my Midwestern manners. I grew up in the outdoors. My family traveled across the United States,

as well as exploring other countries around the world. My father raised me to be who I am today.

I love hunting, fishing, hiking, camping, and exploring everything there is to find outside.

This led me to study Environmental Geosciences at Michigan State University. After graduating, I started in logistics.

I eventually found myself in my desired field, doing ecological restoration on wetlands. Then Covid hit, and I found myself without a job and uncertain about my future.

I wanted something new, innovative, and up with the times. That's when I started looking for software classes. Luckily I found the Eleven Fifty Academy out of Indianapolis.

I have grown to love coding and Software Development. I have always loved puzzles and problem solving, and I think I found the right field. Post graduation from Eleven Fifty, I see myself in a software career for the rest of my life!
